Brownie Recipe

      Prep Work

      • Preheat The Oven To 350 Degrees F
      • Rest The Butter To Room Temperatire
      • Wash Your Hands

Mix Dry Ingredients

  1. In a large mixing bowl, add 2 1/4 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 cup of brown sugar, and 1 cup of cocoa powder. Stir ingredients thoroughly.
  2. Add in 1 tablespoon of baking powder. Also 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  3. Set aside

Mix Wet Ingreedients

  1. In a medium size mixing bowl, brek two large eggs. Whisk until consistent.
  2. Add 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract and half spoon of salt to the egg mixture.
  3. Pour a half cup of milk into the wet mixture.

Combine Mixtures

  1. Place the large mixing bowl with the dry inredients in a stand mixer on low setting.
  2. Slowly pour in wet ingredients until incorporated.
  3. Drop 8 tablespoons of room temperature butter into the mixer. Consistency should be fluid but slow.

Pour And Bake

  1. Grease an 8 by 11 inch pan.
  2. Pour mix into the pan. use a spatula to smooth it out.
  3. Place in oven and bake for 20 minutes or toothpick is clean.
